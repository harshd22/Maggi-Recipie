Maggi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Maggi noodles
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of chopped vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Maggi masala (included in the pack)

Instructions

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles and cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Drain the water and keep the noodles aside.
  4. In the same pan, heat the vegetable oil.
  5. Add the chopped vegetables and saut√© for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked Maggi noodles, salt, black pepper powder, and Maggi masala.
  7. Mix well and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Maggi!
